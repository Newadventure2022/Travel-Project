Valencia

Valencia lies on Spain's southeastern coast, where the Turia River meets the Mediterranean Sea. It's known for its City of Arts and Sciences, with futuristic structures, a planetarium, an oceanarium, and an interactive museum. Valencia also has several beaches and walking trails.
